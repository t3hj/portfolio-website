About

I'm a final-year BSc Computer Science student at Brunel University London, graduating June 2026. My Final Year Project — Local Lens — is a full-stack AI civic-reporting platform that uses Claude vision AI to analyse photographs and auto-fill structured council issue reports, complete with a priority-scoring engine and anti-abuse controls.

Alongside development, I've spent three years as a Robotics & Coding Instructor at RoboThink, teaching Python and hardware integration to 30+ students aged 4–14. That experience sharpened my ability to communicate complex ideas clearly — something I bring directly to code reviews and collaboration.

I'm pursuing graduate roles in software engineering and cybersecurity, backed by Year 3 modules in both disciplines, active TryHackMe practice (Pre-Security and SOC Level 1), and a black belt in Taekwondo — because discipline in the dojo transfers directly to debugging at 2am.
